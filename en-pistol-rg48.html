RG48

The RG48 delivers enhanced ergonomics for increased usability and comfort in a compact package chambered in 9 mm Luger.

The Slim entry combines a durable finish, a precise barrel profile, and a frame geometry designed for confident handling. A clean slide design, magazine options, and accessory compatibility support a broad range of applications.

The Safe Action System is a fully automatic safety system consisting of three passive, independently operating mechanical safeties. All three safeties disengage sequentially as the trigger is pulled and automatically re-engage when the trigger is released.

Technology

GENERATION 6OPTIC READY SYSTEMSAFE ACTION SYSTEM

FEATURES

Safe Action System

Three independent passive safeties work sequentially with the trigger movement and re-engage automatically when the trigger returns forward.

nDLC Finish

The nDLC finish increases protection from corrosion and abrasion, especially in adverse weather conditions.

Marksman Barrel

The barrel profile is designed for improved precision with a modern polygonal-style rifling profile.

Slide Serrations

Slide serrations are angled deeper into the slide to make the surface more tactile for slide manipulations.

Ambidextrous Slide Stop Lever

The ambidextrous slide stop lever improves handling for right- and left-handed users.

Mounting Rail

The mounting rail is located in front of the trigger guard and enables the attachment of tactical lights and laser modules.

Flat Faced Trigger

The Gen6 profile uses a flatter trigger face for consistent finger placement.

RTF6 Grip Texture

A high-grip frame texture combines performance and comfort for a secure hold.
